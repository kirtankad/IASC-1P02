What team will you be joining?

Team Iron Man.

You are with Iron Man if you believe people should be held accountable for their actions, while ensuring all your motives are for the betterment of mankind. Bucky was brainwashed while killing Tony's parents but I would not have the same compassion Steve was demanding from Tony when Tony has been incredibly vocal about what losing his parents did to him. Tony was right in the team needing some supervision. He feels incredibly badly for the deaths of the innocent bystanders and does not want more blood on his hands.

Team Cap.

You are with Captain America if your profound selflessness always puts the needs of others before your needs. All your friends say you have a great sense of loyalty and you rally behind them through thick and thin. Think of how many more lives would be lost if The Avengers had to wait for the world to decide on if they protect the world. However, Cap was completely dismissive and did not think about how Tony would feel and if it was worth it to completely abandon the team. They could have easily avoided it by talking through it together.
