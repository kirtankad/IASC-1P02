WHY DID THE RUSSO BROTHERS DO THIS?

in the process of returning the infinity stones, steve decides to create an alternate timeline with “peggy” and live out his life with her in that timeline while actual steve of that timeline is in ice. okay listen i was cool with the final dance, i think that was definitely needed and it would’ve been a perfect end for steve to do that and return and like give up captain america. but instead they have to destroy ALL OF captain america’s character development, all of peggy carter’s character development, RUIN BUCKY. it is just not something that someone like steve rogers would just ever do & i'm pretty sure peggy carter would've hauled america's a** back to the present because she wouldn't want him to just ruin everything he's worked for & all his relationships.

the directors hardly even gave an explanation for it and its just such a let down and i still don't like how throughout the ENTIRE universe & all their stories, they just completely ignored how bucky had also been tragically pulled away from his family and his timeline. but, no one cared & steve just abandoned him even though Steve ruined his relationship with tony because of bucky & now steve has abandoned bucky for peggy, who's probably moved on. steve rogers deserved a better ending than this.

WHAT ABOUT BUCKY?!


WHAT ABOUT BUCKY?!

Peggy deserved better than to have her future and her marriage chosen for her, her choice taken away by a man she didn’t even know so well on a personal level. yes, steve & peggy are the "greatest" love story ever, blah blah blah. but, peggy carter is a strong woman who would've hated that the guy she lowkey had a crush on 40 years ago, is back in her life & wanting to marry her without asking about what she wants from her life.

it doesnt fit the character for steve or peggy or bucky for them to just abandon everything. steve was finally okay with living in the present & had found a family & he just left them at a point where absolutely everything was uncertain and there was pain. i think steve went back in time because of tony & nat's deaths. he couldnt deal with it, so he left to go back to a time where they were actually alive. rip tony & nat.
