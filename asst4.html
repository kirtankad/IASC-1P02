Who is Chris Evans?

Chris Evans was born in Boston, MA, and raised in Sudbury, MA to a very Italian and very liberal family. His mom is an artistic director at a theatre and his dad is a dentist. He has 3 siblings: 2 sisters and a brother. His brother, Scott, is also an actor and can be seen in shows such as One Life to Live and Grace and Frankie.

Chris graduated from high school and took film classes in NYC. He got his big break in acting with a movie called Not Another Teen Movie and rode that wave to several other movies like Fantastic Four and Scott Pilgrim vs the World. His most prominent role is playing a righteous Marvel character named Steve Rogers, who you may know as Captain America. His latest movie role is playing a privileged jerk in Rian Johnson's whodunit mystery drama, Knives Out.
